# Book of Life Transformation / 命书

⚠️ This content is based on the 命书 from the novel《改运奇书》, for entertainment reference only. It does not constitute any professional advice. | 本内容基于《改运奇书》小说中的命书，仅供娱乐参考，不构成任何专业建议。

## Technique 1: Environmental Purification

**Original Text:** 洒扫庭除，使身不近秽；肃洁仪容，使秽不附身。勿卧于污秽熏天、杂然无序之所，勿寝于阴暗潮湿之地。

**Plain Interpretation:** Keep your surroundings clean and your body well-groomed. Don't sleep in garbage dumps or damp, dark places.

**Chinese:** 易命第一术

**Category:** 环境健康 (Environment & Health)

---

## Technique 2: Living in Rhythm

**Original Text:** 应时而兴，应时而食，应时而作，应时而息。四时有序，心神乃一。

**Plain Interpretation:** Rise when it's time to rise, eat when it's time to eat, work when it's time to work, rest when it's time to rest. Maintaining an orderly rhythm brings inner peace and clarity.

**Chinese:** 易命第二术

**Category:** 环境健康 (Environment & Health)

---

## Technique 3: The Power of Speech

**Original Text:** 言讷而实，语善而真。不泄恶语，不传妄言，不涉谤讥。君子之运，发于唇齿。

**Plain Interpretation:** Speak with honesty and kindness. Don't speak hurtfully, spread lies, or engage in slander and mockery. A gentleman's fortune begins with how he speaks.

**Chinese:** 易命第三术

**Category:** 言行修养 (Speech & Conduct)

---

## Technique 4: Wealth Through Insight, Not Exploitation

**Original Text:** 以见利之明处世，财可求也。以侵害之心得财，悖逆天道，必遭反报：非数倍偿债，即气运消减，身心俱伤。

**Plain Interpretation:** Wealth can be pursued through sharp discernment of opportunities. But gaining wealth through harming others defies cosmic order and will bring retribution: either manifold debts or diminished fortune and harm to body and spirit.

**Chinese:** 易命第四术

**Category:** 财富之道 (Wealth)

## Technique 5: One Thing at a Time

**Original Text:** 旧过过，未未到。事有先后，逐一面之，戒之在贪。

**Plain Interpretation:** Past mistakes are done and cannot be changed. Stop dwelling on them. Face each task before you one at a time, prioritizing what matters most. Beware of greed and haste.

**Chinese:** 易命第五术

**Category:** 言行修养 (Speech & Conduct)

## Technique 6 (Part 1): Sensing Fortune's Arrival

**Plain Interpretation:** When the moment arrives and fortune stirs, sense it and go with the flow. When you act in harmony with the Dao, it's like sailing with the wind—nothing can stop you.

**Chinese:** 易命第六术（其一）

**Category:** 做事智慧 (Timing & Action)

## Technique 6 (Part 2): The Path That Feels Right

**Plain Interpretation:** Most people chase shortcuts without realizing these paths are jammed with travelers, while the broad, smooth road lies empty. If something feels effortless and natural to you, that's your true path.

## Technique 7: The Art of Non-Attachment

## Technique 8: Knowing When to Hold On

**Original Text:** 业之道，旧业勿轻弃。虽利薄，然根基之所系，存身之本也。若新业既成，根基稳固，方可择之而从。若二者得兼，善之善者也。

**Plain Interpretation:** Never abandon your old venture prematurely. Even if profits are thin, it's your foundation and means of survival. Only after your new venture is stable should you consider switching. Having both is ideal.

**Chinese:** 易命第八术

**Category:** 处世哲学 (Worldly Wisdom)

## Technique 9: Reading Strangers

**Original Text:** 初遇生人，若生厌憎，心神不安，速避之。见使君怡然者，可近而交，然须慎察其行。必试其信义，此乃立世之本。

**Plain Interpretation:** When meeting strangers, if you feel instant dislike or discomfort, stay away. If someone puts you at ease, you may approach, but watch their actions carefully. Test their trustworthiness—this is the foundation of navigating the world. Retreat immediately if they prove dishonest.

**Chinese:** 易命第九术

**Category:** 人际之道 (Interpersonal)

## Technique 10: Recognizing Benefactors

**Original Text:** 父母者，天授贵人也；困厄中施以援手者，贵人也；指迷津于惘途者，贵人也；甘苦与共之友，贵人也；生死相托之夫妻，互为贵人也。然贵人非固守一世，乃如云水流转不息。当明眸察周遭之贵气，承其瑞意，则如握机缘之枢机。

**Plain Interpretation:** Parents are heaven-sent benefactors; so are those who help in hardship, guide you through confusion, share joy and sorrow, or entrust their lives to you. But benefactors don't remain fixed—they flow like clouds and water. Recognize the blessings around you and embrace them to seize opportunity.

**Chinese:** 易命第十术

**Category:** 处世哲学 (Worldly Wisdom)

## Technique 11: The Virtue of Silence

**Original Text:** 吾观处世之道，事未成勿泄于未预者，利既获勿宣于不知者。倘炫其功，恐招非议而损其益。

**Plain Interpretation:** Keep your unfinished plans private from outsiders and your gains unknown to the uninformed. Bragging about achievements invites unwanted attention and trouble that could diminish your benefits.

**Chinese:** 易命第十一术

**Category:** 做事智慧 (Timing & Action)

## Technique 13: Seeing True Character

**Original Text:** 识人者，当涤尽浮光，弃置衔冕，略其形骸，屏绝人议。惟观其言行之微，察其举止之细，则彼若素缣一卷，自将仁善鄙诈，贞邪曲直，书而示汝矣。

**Plain Interpretation:** To truly understand someone, strip away their glamour, titles, status, appearance, and others' opinions. Observe the smallest details of their words and actions. Like an unrolled white silk scroll, they will reveal themselves—whether kind or cunning, loyal or deceitful, upright or crooked.

**Chinese:** 易命第十三术

**Category:** 处世哲学 (Worldly Wisdom)

## Technique 14: Vigilance in Partnerships

**Original Text:** 御人当以疑始，必待其事毕。绳可束其身，岂能羁其志？人为灵长，旦暮异焉，唯以疑目察之，乃见其纤隐之变。

**Plain Interpretation:** Approach partnerships with suspicion from the start until completion. You can bind someone's body but not their mind. People are ever-changing—only constant vigilance reveals subtle shifts they've hidden.

**Chinese:** 易命第十四术

**Category:** 处世哲学 (Worldly Wisdom)

## Technique 15: Three Measures of Success

**Original Text:** 判事之成否，其下者取利，其次者取鉴，其至者观乎气运之蓄。

**Plain Interpretation:** Judging success has three levels: the lowest judges by profit, the middle by lessons learned, and the highest by whether you've accumulated fortune. Money can be spent, experience can become obsolete, but accumulated fortune shapes your long-term trajectory.

**Chinese:** 易命第十五术

**Category:** 做事智慧 (Timing & Action)

## Technique 16: Three Types of People

**Original Text:** 交游之众，当分三等：其一唯利是图，毋涉情义；其二唯情是守，毋涉利欲；此二者不可逾，逾则必遭其咎。其三情利皆可谋。得此辈愈众，则气运愈昌。

**Plain Interpretation:** People around you fall into three categories: those who only want profit (keep emotions out), those who only value feelings (keep money out), and never cross these boundaries. The third type combines both naturally. The more such people you gather, the more your fortune grows.

**Chinese:** 易命第十六术

**Category:** 做事智慧 (Timing & Action)

## Technique 18: The Triad of Successful Collaboration

**Plain Interpretation:** For joint endeavors to succeed: plan thoroughly with vision, give your all, and most importantly, forget self-interest. With these three, you'll achieve eight or nine times out of ten. The rest is heavenly will.

## Technique 41 (Part 1): All-In and Thick-Faced

**Original Text:** 豁尽所有乃制胜之枢，面厚如革实为初基。事成则万口争颂，功败则形迹俱冥。故无须怀讥诮之虞于方寸，洞明此理者，天岂肯薄待之？昔有破釜沉舟之志，而世惟传奏凯之歌，谁见折戟沉沙之迹？诚能心无滞碍，行不疑迟，既悟玄机，气运自随。

**Plain Interpretation:** Going all-in is the key to victory; thick-skinnedness is the starting point. Success brings praise, failure erases memory. Don't fear mockery. Those who understand this won't be mistreated by heaven. Only Xiang Yu's triumphant battles are remembered, not his fallen soldiers. Act without hesitation once you grasp the truth, and fortune follows.

**Chinese:** 易命第四十一术（其一）

**Category:** 做事智慧 (Timing & Action)
