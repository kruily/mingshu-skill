# Action Checklist Generation Template

## Purpose
Transform interpreted technique wisdom into concrete, executable daily practices. Generate practice checklists that are:
- **Specific and measurable** — clear completion criteria
- **Realistic for modern life** — achievable in 5-15 minute blocks
- **Progressively challenging** — build capacity over time
- **Encouraging, not overwhelming** — celebrate small wins

## Core Principle
> Actions should be practical, not abstract philosophy. Meet people where they are.

---

## Practice Notes

### Tone Guidance
- **Use encouraging language:** "Begin where you are" not "You must"
- **Acknowledge effort:** "Taking this step shows courage"
- **Normalize setbacks:** "Missing one day doesn't break the practice"
- **Celebrate progress:** "Each time you do this, you're building fortune"

### Framing Tips
| Instead of | Use This |
|------------|----------|
| "You should never..." | "Consider trying..." |
| "Failure to do this will..." | "Notice how this helps..." |
| "You must do X every day" | "Even 5 minutes of X builds the habit" |
| "Stop doing Y" | "When you catch yourself doing Y, pause and redirect" |

### Making Steps Specific & Measurable
1. **Use verbs:** Do, practice, notice, reflect, pause, breathe
2. **Add numbers:** 3 times, 5 minutes, 2 times per day, 30 seconds
3. **Define completion:** "I did X when Y happened" or "X count reached"
4. **Make it observable:** What you see, hear, feel externally

### Realistic Modern Life Check
- [ ] Can someone do this in under 15 minutes?
- [ ] Does this fit into existing routines (morning, commute, lunch, evening)?
- [ ] Does this require special equipment or location? (If yes, simplify)
- [ ] Is there a "micro version" for busy days?

### Progressive Challenge Framework
```
Week 1-2: Foundation (do the thing, even imperfectly)
Week 3-4: Consistency (do the thing reliably)
Week 5-6: Quality (do the thing with attention)
Week 7+:   Integration (thing becomes natural)
```

## Example: 易命第三术 (言语修养)

### Difficulty Summary
**Problem:** 习惯性说伤人的话，事后后悔
**Root Cause:** 情绪一来，嘴比脑子快；没建立"说话前停顿"的习惯
**Technique Match:** 易命第三术 — 言讷而实，语善而真

### Action Checklist

#### 🟢 基础练习
| # | Action | Duration | Success Criteria |
|---|--------|----------|------------------|
| 1 | 生气时，先深呼吸3次再开口 | 30 seconds | 做到"停顿"再说话 |
| 2 | 说"不"之后，加一句解释 | 1 minute | 拒绝时不伤人 |
| 3 | 每天睡前回顾：今天说了哪3句让自己后悔的话 | 5 minutes | 完成记录 |

**Why these work:** 停顿打破自动化反应；记录建立觉察

#### 🟡 进阶练习
| # | Practice | Frequency | Success Criteria |
|---|----------|-----------|------------------|
| 1 | 设置手机提醒："说话前停顿" | 每次想开口时 | 每周有3次以上使用提醒 |
| 2 | 对镜子练习拒绝话术3种 | 每天2分钟 | 能流利说不伤人的"不" |
| 3 | 主动夸1个同事/朋友具体的事 | 每天1次 | 完成即打勾 |

**Why these build:** 建立新习惯的肌肉记忆；正向语言替换负向模式

#### 🔵 整合练习
| # | Mindset Shift | Reflection Prompt | Growth Indicator |
|---|---------------|-------------------|------------------|
| 1 | 语言是气运的入口 | "今天我的话是给了别人力量还是消耗了能量？" | 后悔次数减少 |
| 2 | 善意但不软弱 | "这件事我真的想答应吗？还是害怕冲突？" | 能区分"真愿意"和"怕拒绝" |

**Why this matters:** 言语塑造现实，每句话都是选择

### Practice Notes
> 记住：哪怕只做到1次停顿，都是巨大的进步。气运的改变不是一夜之间，而是每一次小小的正确选择累积而来。

## Validation Checklist

Before presenting actions, verify:
- [ ] Each action has clear time estimate
- [ ] Each action has measurable completion criteria
- [ ] Actions are diverse (not all thinking, not all doing)
- [ ] Tone is encouraging, not prescriptive
- [ ] No action requires more than 15 minutes per day for foundation
- [ ] There's a "micro version" for overwhelming days
- [ ] Progress is visible/self-evident

---
